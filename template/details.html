Ticket Star

Ticket Star is India's biggest online movie and events ticketing brand. The website caters to ticket sales for movies, plays, concerts and sporting events via the online platform. Launched in 2007, it is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It additionally has offices in New Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai and Kolkata.
